Lagos Chamber of Commerce

Supporting business growth and economic development in Lagos.

Site Purpose

The Lagos Chamber of Commerce website aims to:

  • Promote Lagos businesses and economic opportunities.
  • Provide networking and event details for business owners.
  • Offer resources for entrepreneurs and investors.

Scenarios

Visitors to this site may ask:

  • What business networking events are happening in Lagos this month?
  • How do I register my business with the chamber?
  • Where can I find a directory of local businesses?

Color Scheme

Primary color: Deep Blue (#002147) (Headings, Navbar).

Accent color: Vibrant Yellow (#FFD700) (Highlights, Buttons).

Typography

Headings: Roboto

Body text: Open Sans
